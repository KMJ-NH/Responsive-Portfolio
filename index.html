About Me

I'm enrolled in the University of New Hampshire's coding Boot Camp with a plan to become a web devloper. I have a background working with data in various ways, and working as a SAS programmer to pull raw data files, manipulate the data, and then populate SQL tables with my manipulated data to be used in report generation.

In my earlier professional life I used my degree in psychology to work with chronically mentally ill adults in the community, and I developed and carried out behavior modification techniques with physically agressive brain-injured adults within a rehabilitation center environment. While working in the rehab center, I also worked as a research assistant to a behavioral neurologist by pulling data from medical charts for a medication study he was doing.
